Hearing

Hearing is like the middle brother in a family, always there but kinda not. It is probably the second sense we use most while surfing the net (or using technology in general), yet we never pay much attention to it. With this one exception perhaps, of when sound is what we are actively searching for. And yet there it is, always there. Guiding us through our little adventures, with little signals to alert us when we stray, be it trying to delete characters from an already empty form, or when we we falter in our faith an try to leave those places that, so cordially, insist on asking us if we so surely want to leave this place and pass on the chance of winning a new iPad.

Contrary to all this, of course, would be the experience to those unfortunate to have lost their sight, as sound is precisely their way of browsing the tubes. (Does anybody call it that anymore?)
