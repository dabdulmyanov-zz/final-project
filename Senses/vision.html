Sight

Oh sight. Sight is the sense, isn't it? Our eyes are the portal to this awe inspiring pile of human knowledge and interaction that we call the Internet. Everything we do goes through our eyes first, specially on the web. A powerfull tool, but a limitation nevertheless.

There is so much our eyes can see at a single moment of our life, and that is definitively not enough to process all the information available and relevant to us at any given time. Because that's the issue, isn't it? Before the internet there was a constant limit to information available, but now the problem is that we have so much of it that we need to make decisions about what to process and what not. And one of the main reasons to that is because our vast collective intelligence is mostly limited to pass through this one funnel we call sight, while the rest of or body remains limp and inactive.
