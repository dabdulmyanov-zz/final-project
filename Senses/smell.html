Smell

Smell... Whan can be said about smell. Hilarity ensues when you try to imagine a smelly website, but once that feeling passes, the question remains. What if?

I hold no doubt that, however farfetched the concept may seem, the access to information will continue evolving untill in allows all senses to benefit from it. How, however, is a matter of interesting speculation. Local synthesyzers on each terminal? Or perhaps a more invasive implanted nodes that would simulate the reaction to our receptors? I suppose a full trilogy of science fiction could be written about this topic (and probably achieve a bestseller ranking, considering what you can find nowadays on those lists anyways) but I would gladly defer the topic to those potential writers. We have other things to do, do we not?
